Banking Customers Portfolio

🎯 Optimize Account Portfolio Performance

Analyze which account types contribute the highest balances and focus on promoting those products. The objective is to increase total deposits by encouraging customers to shift toward high-value account types through targeted campaigns, better interest rates, or bundled services. This also helps in improving liquidity management and overall financial stability.

⚠️ Business Problems

a. Low contribution from certain account types reduces overall deposit growth and impacts financial stability.
b. High-value customers are concentrated in limited account types, increasing dependency risk.
c. Lack of visibility into customer spending behavior limits personalization strategies.
d. Declining quarterly investments from some brokers impacts overall portfolio growth.
e. Seasonal investment fluctuations create challenges in financial forecasting.

📊 Analysis Performed

* A small number of account types contribute disproportionately to total balances (Pareto effect).
* Category-wise contribution varies significantly across merchants, indicating different business models.
* Some brokers show declining QoQ trends—early warning for churn or disengagement.
* Revenue and investment concentration across few entities increases business risk.

🛠️ Business Solutions
* Launch targeted campaigns to shift customers toward high-balance account types (premium benefits, better interest rates).
* Promote high-margin categories with strategic discounts and bundled offers.
* Build recommendation systems based on spending patterns (category & merchant level).
* Introduce performance-based incentives for brokers (quarterly bonuses, rankings).	
* Use historical quarterly trends to forecast investments and allocate resources.
